Collaboration in CHC

In many areas, developers and conservationists are at odds with one another.  And in many areas, we see the unfortunate result of this adversarial relationship.  In the CHC the developers and conservationists are working together to create a model community that preserves the environment and enhances the quality of life for residents, old and new.
